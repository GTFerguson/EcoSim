# World Biomes

EcoSim features **22 distinct biomes** based on the Whittaker classification system. Each biome has unique temperature, moisture, and environmental characteristics that affect which creatures and plants can survive there.

---

## Understanding Biomes

A **biome** is a climate zone with characteristic plant and animal life. In EcoSim, biomes are determined by two factors:

- **Temperature** - How hot or cold the region is
- **Moisture** - How wet or dry the region is

The combination of these creates a unique environment!

## Biome Boundaries (Ecotones)

Where biomes meet, you'll find **transition zones** called ecotones. These areas:

- Blend characteristics of both biomes
- May have unique species diversity
- Temperature and moisture change gradually

### Example Transitions

| From | To | Transition Zone |
|------|-----|----------------|
| Desert | Savanna | Semi-arid scrubland |
| Tundra | Boreal | Sparse treeline |
| Rainforest | Seasonal | Drier forest edges |

## Recognizing Biomes Visually

### Color Guide

| Color | Likely Biome |
|-------|--------------|
| **Dark Green** | Rainforest, Dense Forest |
| **Light Green** | Grassland, Meadow |
| **Yellow-Green** | Savanna, Dry Grassland |
| **Yellow/Tan** | Desert, Semi-Arid |
| **White/Light Gray** | Ice, Tundra |
| **Blue** | Water |
| **Brown/Gray** | Mountains, Rocky |

### Temperature Indicators

- **Cold biomes:** Lighter, whiter tones
- **Hot biomes:** Yellower, browner tones
- **Wet biomes:** Darker, richer greens

## Biome Diversity Tips

### For Creature Success

1. **Stay in your comfort zone** - Match your creature's temperature tolerance to the biome
2. **Watch transitions** - Biome edges can be dangerous for unprepared creatures
3. **Follow the food** - Herbivores should stick to plant-rich biomes

### For Evolution

- Creatures that spread across biomes will develop different adaptations
- Population isolation by biomes can lead to speciation
- Harsh biomes select for specific traits
