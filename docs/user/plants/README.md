# Understanding Plants

Plants are the foundation of the EcoSim ecosystem. They convert sunlight into energy, feed herbivores, spread their seeds, and evolve defenses against being eaten.

---

## What Are Plants?

In EcoSim, plants are living organisms with:

- **Genes** that control their traits (size, toxins, fruit production)
- **Growth cycles** from seed to mature plant
- **Reproduction** through various seed dispersal methods
- **Defenses** against herbivores (thorns, toxins)
- **The ability to evolve** across generations

Plants don't move (usually!), but they're far from passive.

Plants aren't helpless! They can evolve defenses:

## Common Questions

### Why did this plant die?

Plants die from:
- **Old age** (reached lifespan)
- **Herbivore damage** (eaten completely)
- **Environmental stress** (wrong conditions)

### Why don't plants spread to this area?

Possible reasons:
- Seeds don't reach there (dispersal limited)
- Environment unsuitable (wrong terrain)
- Herbivores eat all new plants
- No parent plants nearby

### Can plants move?

Normally no, but through evolution:
- Dormant mobility genes exist
- Rare mutations could activate them
- Theoretically possible to evolve mobile plants!
